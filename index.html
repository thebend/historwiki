Historwiki

Wiki-style interactive history of the world map-timeline

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Purpose

The idea is to provide an interactive timeline-integrated mapping tool that filters content based on the zoom and speed plus optional category tags, allowing people to better understand the context of events. Content will be user-contributed and voted up/down in relevance (voting system might be subject to revision later) with a strong wiki-like contribution style.
